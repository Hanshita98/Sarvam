About Us | Sarvam Gruh Udhoyog

Our Story

Pure. Traditional. Homemade.

Sarvam Gruh Udhoyog was born from a simple belief — good food doesn’t need preservatives, machines, or shortcuts.

Rooted in the rich culinary traditions of Kutch, Gujarat, Sarvam Gruh Udhoyog is a homegrown initiative dedicated to delivering authentic homemade snacks prepared with care, hygiene, and love.

Every product is handcrafted in small batches using traditional recipes, ensuring quality, taste, and purity in every bite.

From our kitchen to your home, we bring flavors that feel familiar, comforting, and truly Indian.
